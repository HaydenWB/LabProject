Star Wars: An Encyclopedia

Star Wars is an American science-fiction fantasy series created by George Lucas in 1977. The series has spawned multiple movies, television shows, books, comics, games, videogames, theme parks, and works of art. The Star Wars universe sees humans from a long long time ago interacting with various humanoid and non-humanoid species, sentient droids, and a mysterious magical entity known as "The Force."

The Star Wars franchise is the 5th highest grossing franchise in the world. It is worth more thant 70 billion USD. Fans of the franchise can be found all across the world.
